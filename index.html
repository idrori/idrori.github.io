Artificial General Intelligence

Developing mathematical foundations for safe AGI systems with superhuman-level reasoning across domains.

Superhuman Mathematical Reasoning

Self-Improving Systems

Research on autonomously evolving AI systems that continuously improve themselves without losing alignment.

Autonomous Scientific Discovery

Accelerating scientific research through automated hypothesis generation and experimentation.

Continual Meta-Learning

Systems that learn to learn, rapidly adapting to new domains with minimal data while retaining prior knowledge.

AI for Education

Transforming education by personalized learning systems with human oversight.

Human-AI Coevolution

Developing and understanding human-AI interaction and collaboration workflows and their economic intelligence.
